About The Show

Hey Hey 16K is the brand new two man rock opera from MJ Hibbett (and Steve), the people who gave the world Dinosaur Planet, Moon Horse VS The Mars Men Of Jupiter and Total Hero Team.

The show features a pile of brand new songs alongside some old favourites like 'I Come From The Fens' and, of course, 'Hey Hey 16K'. It's an hour's worth of singing, dancing and titting around covering subjects such as time travel, the history of home computing, growing up, the nature of historical accuracy and what exactly it means to be 'normal'.

Come and see it, it'll be great!
